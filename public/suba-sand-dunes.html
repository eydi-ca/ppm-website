Suba Sand Dunes, Ilocos Norte

The perfect backdrop for rugged, adventurous, and stunning photography.

Explore the Desert of the North

The Suba Sand Dunes in Ilocos Norte offer a unique, otherworldly landscape perfect for dramatic and memorable photoshoots. Often described as a mini-desert, this location provides vast stretches of rolling sand hills against the backdrop of the ocean.

Our photographers specialize in using the dynamic light and rugged textures of the dunes to create powerful images, whether you're seeking a pre-nuptial shoot, adventure portraits, or simple travel photos. The experience is often complemented by exciting 4x4 rides and sandboarding, adding an element of thrilling activity to your session.

Booking a package here ensures you get not just stunning photos, but also local guidance to capture the best light during sunrise or sunset, making your sand dunes adventure truly unforgettable.

Location Key Details

  • Location: Suba, Paoay, Ilocos Norte
  • Best Time: Sunrise (5:30 AM) or Sunset (4:00 PM)
  • Activity: 4x4 Ride, Sand Boarding, Photography
  • Recommended Shoot: Adventure Portraits
  • Accessibility: Moderate (requires a 4x4 vehicle)
